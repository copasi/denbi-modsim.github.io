## Workshop Series de.NBI-SysBio
Join us for an exciting workshop series where we will explore various topics in systems biology. Each workshop will provide hands-on training and practical examples to enhance your understanding of the subject matter. 


* (in person) Workshop 1: [COPASI](./copasi/): Advanced Modelling with COPASI
* (virtual) Workshop 2: [CellNetAnalyzer/CNApy](./cna/):Constraint-based modeling and design of metabolic networks with CellNetAnalyzer and CNApy.
